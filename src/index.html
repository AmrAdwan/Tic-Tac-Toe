Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to the classic game of Tic Tac Toe!

Instructions:

  • Click on any empty square to make your move.
  • The first player is always 'X'.
  • The game will alternate turns between you and the computer.
  • The first to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) wins the game.
  • If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
